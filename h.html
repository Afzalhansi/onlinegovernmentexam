How to Fill Out the Application Form

Create an Account: Start by creating a secure account on our platform and provide the necessary personal details.
Select Exam: Browse through the available exams, choose the desired one, and review the eligibility criteria.
Complete Application: Fill out the application form with accurate information, including personal details, exam preferences, and any additional requirements Submit Documents: Upload any required documents, such as identification proof or educational certificates.
Review and Confirm: Double-check the information provided, ensure its accuracy, and submit the application.
Double-Check Your Entries :Review your information before submission to ensure accuracy and avoid any errors
